# Amelia Earhart Playhouse

**The Amelia Earhart Playhouse** offers live theatrical productions including comedies, dramas, musicals, one-act plays, and children’s theater.

The Playhouse has transitioned to a new location on Lower Hainerberg between The Heart of Seoul and the WCSC Thrift Shop.

Our award-winning productions are second to none, with a unique, intimate theater setting and talented show casts. We welcome your participation onstage, backstage, or as an audience member.

Playhouse Rules and Regulations

There is absolutely no eating, drinking, smoking, picture-taking or cell phone use during a performance. Additionally, certain shows are intended for mature audiences -- please use our ratings system to decide if a show is appropriate viewing for your children.

Ticket Information

Purchase tickets at the Wiesbaden Arts and Crafts Center (open Tuesdays to Saturdays from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. -- call civ 0611-143-548-9838) or through Webtrac: [https://webtrac.mwr.army.mil/webtrac103/wbwsc/wiesbadenrectrac.wsc/wbsplash.html?wbp=1](https://webtrac.mwr.army.mil/webtrac103/wbwsc/wiesbadenrectrac.wsc/wbsplash.html?wbp=1)
