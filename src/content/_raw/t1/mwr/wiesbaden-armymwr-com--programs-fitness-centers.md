***** Who Can Use the Fitness Centers? *****
    * Active-duty, Reserve, and National Guard service members
    * Retirees 
    * Family members 
    * DoW Civilians 
    * Authorized contractors with approved identification
    * Guests accompanied by an authorized patron 
 
***** Guest Policy *****
    * Guests must be accompanied by an authorized patron at all times
    * You can bring up to two guests per visit
    * Guests must show valid photo ID and sign in at the front desk
    * Some locations may have restrictions. Check with your local fitness center
[fitness_center_class.jpg]
***** Why Use Army MWR Fitness Centers? *****
    * They’re convenient. On-post locations make it easy to pop in for a workout
    * They’re affordable. Most services are free or low-cost
    * They’re comprehensive. From cardio and strength training to group classes and personal
      training, there’s something for everyone
    * They’re supportive. Fellow DoW patrons provide the charge your workout needs
