| U.S. Army Garrison Wiesbaden ******

**** Clay Kaserne Bravo Box set to enhance self-help services ****
In an effort to improve accessibility and streamline housing self-help services, United States Army
Garrison Wiesbaden has launched the Bravo Box, offering a convenient solution for minor repairs and
maintenance needs. Located across the parking lot from the Re-Use Center, this new container is
designed to give the community another option to replenish common housing maintenance items. 
Read_more.
DPW is the garrison's primary element for maintenance of the installation, infrastructure and the
environment. It encompasses the review of work classification and technical adequacy of all
projects; reviews and validates submission of  Military Construction projects for all construction
and renovations occurring on the installation; manages IMCOM's Sustainment, Restoration and
Moderation projects approved and funded for each fiscal year; updates real property assets,
allowances, requirements and programmed construction data in a variety of databases; provides day-
to-day sustainment and maintenance support for the installation tenant activities; monitors and
ensures all work and military-training activities are accomplished in compliance with federal and
host nation environmental requirements and regulations; and provides oversight for all quality of
life requirements for service members and their Families residing on the installation and in the
local community.
DPW consists of five divisions:  Business Operations and Integration,
Housing, Environmental, Engineering, Master Planning, and Operations and Maintenance.  
***** Public Works Divisions *****
Business Operations Integration Division 
The Business Operations Integration Division provides management of directorate resources,
information technology, human resources and organizational strategic planning along with financial
planning, analysis, programming support and industrial engineering services.
    * Customer_Service_Center_-_Requesting_Repairs
Master Planning Division 
The Master Planning Office develops and maintains Real Property Master Plans, conducts Real Property
Planning Board meetings, provides Major Construction Programming Services, provides maximum
utilization of Facilities Services, provides Real Property Accountability Services, grants use of
Army Real Property and disposes of real estate, facilities, and real estate interests 
    * Customer_Service_Center_-_Space_Requests
Operations & Maintenance Division
O&M supports the USAG Wiesbaden mission through effective use of energy, providing and maintaining
sustainable facilities and infrastructure, and emphasizing a culture of safety.
    * Self-Help_Store
    * Trash_and_Recycling
    * Customer_Service_Center_-_Requesting_Repairs
Engineering Division
To provide engineering support and construction project management to the U.S. Army Garrison. DPW's
Engineering Division plans, organizes, coordinates and oversees master planning, execution of
design, construction and real property management.
Housing_Division 
The Housing Division takes care of in-processing and out-processing for service members, civilians
and their Families living on and off post. Mandatory assignment to government-controlled housing is
in effect for service members. 
Environmental_Division
The Environmental Division provides environmental guidance, support and liaison services to those
who live, work and train on the installation. Their role is the ensure the Wiesbaden military
community complies with all necessary environmental requirements.
