# Contact    \| U.S. Army Garrison Wiesbaden

### Dialing Outside Germany

To dial a German commercial telephone number from within the United States: **Dial 011 49 and drop the first 0 of the commercial number**.

To dial a German commercial telephone number from within Europe, but outside of Germany: **Dial 0049 and drop the first 0 of the commercial number**.

To dial a Defense Switched Network number from outside of Europe: **Dial 314 plus the seven digit DSN**.
