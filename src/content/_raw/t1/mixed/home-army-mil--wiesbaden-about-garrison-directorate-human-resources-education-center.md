# Education Center   \| U.S. Army Garrison Wiesbaden

The Education Staff provides quality services and programs to active duty, retirees, civilians and family members. Professional guidance counselors provide a full range of adult and continuing education counseling. Individual counseling is available during normal duty hours on a walk-in basis.

### Educational Institutions

The following institutions have face-to-face classes available for ID card holders. These institutions have contracts with the military to provide school representatives to assist with enrollment and support. They also provide online classes and degree plans.

- [Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University](http://worldwide.erau.edu/locations/wiesbaden/) (ERAU)
- [University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC)](https://europe.umgc.edu/locations/germany-wiesbaden.cfm)

### Army Continuing Education System (ACES) Testing Services

- Academic Skills Assessment: TABE test - Test of Adult Basic Education
- Army Personnel Testing: ASVAB/AFCT, DLAB, DLPT, SIFT, OPI

_To schedule an APT exam [usarmy.wiesbaden-germany.id-europe.mbx.education-testing@army.mil](mailto:usarmy.wiesbaden-germany.id-europe.mbx.education-testing@army.mil)_
- Graduate School Exams- information only for GRE
