### Request Support

To submit an event or activity for publicity consideration on U.S. Army Wiesbaden communication platforms, you must first submit your event to the USAG Wiesbaden Public Affairs Office **at least 30 days prior to your event**. Fillable PDF form can be viewed and downloaded [here](https://home.army.mil/wiesbaden/download_file/view/1831/326).

Public Affairs does not provide photo support for ceremonies or events except for covering them for official garrison support. To request photo or video documentation of ceremonies, please make a request to the [Wiesbaden Training Support Center](https://vios.army.mil/) [.](http://www.vios.army.mil/)

To request AFN publicity or coverage, visit the [AFN Wiesbaden website](http://www.afneurope.net/Stations/Wiesbaden.aspx).

For queries about U.S. Army Europe & Africa, contact the [U.S. ARMY EUROPE & AFRICA PUBLIC AFFAIRS OFFICE](https://www.europeafrica.army.mil/)

### Submit an Article for Publication

Staffing and time constraints prevent us from attending every function or event in person. As well, every function or event is not necessarily newsworthy. TheGarrison welcomes articles and announcement submissions from outside unit public affairs representatives and volunteer writers.

Send timely articles to our group [email box](mailto:usarmy.wiesbaden.id-europe.mbx.public-affairs-office@army.mil?subject=Article%20for%20Submission)
