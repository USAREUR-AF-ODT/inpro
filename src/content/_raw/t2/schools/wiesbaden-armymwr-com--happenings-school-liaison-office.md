# School Liaison Office

![](https://wiesbaden.armymwr.com/application/files/2717/6042/9312/WI-SLO-TeensSchool.jpg)

The School Liaison Office offers a buffet of information and assistance for transitioning students to help families in their for moves to and from U.S. Army Garrison Wiesbaden. The School Liaison Officer is here to help connect students, parents, schools and local units and organizations in supporting the education of military and civilian youth overseas.

Visit the School Liaison Office page for links, resources and information on installation DoDEA schools, home-schooling and off-base public German and private international schools.
