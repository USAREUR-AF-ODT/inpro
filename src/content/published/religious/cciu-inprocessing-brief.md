 WHO ARE WE?
  -U.S. Army Criminal Investigation Command, Computer Crime Investigative Unit


• Conduct worldwide criminal investigations and digital forensic
  examinations of computer intrusions, national security threats, and
  related malicious activities:
     - involving U.S. Army networks, systems, or data; or
     - committed by Department of Defense (DoD) personnel
• Conduct proactive cybercrime prevention surveys and other
  preventive policing services in support of the U.S. Army’s
  Cybersecurity, Insider Threat, and Protection Programs


• WE PROTECT YOU!

        know.
