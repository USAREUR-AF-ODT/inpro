## Strong Families. Safe homes. Help when you need it.

Military life comes with unique challenges, and sometimes those challenges can put stress on relationships and Families. The Family Advocacy Program (FAP) exists to support Soldiers and Families through difficult times. Whether it’s building stronger parenting skills, learning new ways to manage stress, or finding safety from abuse, we’re here to help.

![Service member and family unpacking a car](https://wiesbaden.armymwr.com/application/files/8317/6843/2276/Family_PSC_Move_optimized.jpg)

Our services are free, private, and focused on prevention and support. If you ever feel unsafe, call 911 right away. You can also reach the [Family Advocacy Hotline](https://wiesbaden.armymwr.com/programs/family-advocacy-program/conact) any time of day or call the National Domestic Violence Hotline at [+1(800)799-7233](tel:+18007997233).
