## FAQs

What is MilitaryINSTALLATIONS?

MilitaryINSTALLATIONS is an online directory from Military OneSource that provides information about U.S. military bases worldwide, for every service branch. Use it to find overviews of local installation features, contacts for military and family support programs and services, maps and directions, check-in procedures and other important installation information.

Who can use MilitaryINSTALLATIONS?

Though it’s designed for service members and military families, anyone can use MilitaryINSTALLATIONS.

What kind of information is available on MilitaryINSTALLATIONS?

MilitaryINSTALLATIONS provides detailed, up-to-date contact information for programs and services, state resources, maps and directions, links to comprehensive location overviews and important information for military installations worldwide, including:

- Check-in procedures and base essentials
- Relocation and deployment assistance
- Moving resources and assistance
- Child and youth services
- Housing
- Overseas schools
- School liaisons
- The Exceptional Family Member Program
- Personal financial management services

MilitaryINSTALLATIONS enables users to quickly learn the local features of the base and access the resources and services they need. You can also create a customized, downloadable booklet of useful information about an installation.
