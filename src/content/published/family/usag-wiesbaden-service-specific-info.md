## Subtopics

Post Services

USAG Wiesbaden offers many installation services.

MWR offers numerous indoor and outdoor recreational facilities and clubs, including outdoor recreation, library, bowling center, Arts and Crafts, golf, gym and theater.

For **a list of the Family and MWR available services** please check the [USAG Wiesbaden MWR](https://wiesbaden.armymwr.com/programs) website

- There is one (1) [commissary](https://www.commissaries.com/shopping/store-locations/wiesbaden) and an AAFES store located in Hainerberg Housing.
- On post financial institutions include Community Bank and Andrews Federal Credit Union both in Clay Kaserned and Hainerberg Housing.

Please check the [Wiesbaden Newcommers website](https://home.army.mil/wiesbaden/index.php/my-fort/newcomers) to learn more on the resources available to you during your PCS.

Army Community Service

Please also check them on Facebook, USAG Wiesbaden ACS.

ACS provides a wide array of community programs.

Please also check the ACS Facebook page for the latest events and classes. (USAG Wiesbaden ACS)

[USAG Wiesbaden ACS](https://wiesbaden.armymwr.com/categories/community-support)

They can be reached at 011-49-611-143-548-9201 or 9202 or DSN 314-548-9201/9202,

Hours of operation:

From 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Mondays through Fridays.

Except Thursdays, from 1:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.
