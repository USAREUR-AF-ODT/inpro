## What

Army Community Service is the central family-support hub for USAG Wiesbaden. What it offers:

- **Relocation Readiness Program (RRP)**; spouse orientation, host-nation orientation, newcomer briefings
- **Spouse Sponsorship**; pairs you with a local spouse volunteer who's been through PCS here
- **Lending Closet**; free 30-day loan of essential household items while you wait for HHG delivery (dishes, pots, toaster, iron, kitchen basics; 15-day loan for phones/GPS)
- **Financial Readiness Program (FRP)**; budget counseling, debt, entitlements
- **Family Advocacy Program (FAP)**; domestic violence prevention, new-parent support
- **Employment Readiness Program (ERP)**; spouse employment, resume help, job listings
- **Exceptional Family Member Program (EFMP) Liaison**; local EFMP advocacy, respite care referrals
- **Mobilization & Deployment Readiness**
- **AER (Army Emergency Relief)**; short-term emergency loans for Soldiers

Services are available to Active Duty, National Guard/Reserve, DoD civilians, contractors, and their dependents. Surviving family members of fallen military personnel also qualify.

## Where

**Mississippi Straße 22, Bldg 7790, Hainerberg Housing.** Parking available on-site. CAC required for base entry.

The multipurpose room hosts newcomer briefings every 3rd Tuesday (0900-1600), which includes a walking tour of downtown Wiesbaden.

## When

- **Monday / Tuesday / Wednesday / Friday**: 0800-1700
- **Thursday**: appointments only until 1300
- **Closed**: U.S. federal holidays
- **Newcomer Orientation**: 3rd Tuesday of each month, 0900-1600

## Gotchas

- **Spouse sponsorship is separate from your sponsor-in-unit assignment.** The unit sponsor handles logistics; the ACS spouse sponsor provides peer-to-peer support and local knowledge. Use both.
- **Thursday is appointments only** after 1300; don't show up at 1400 for a walk-in.
- **Lending closet bins are inventoried on return.** Missing items mean you pay replacement; return items cleaned, assembled, and complete.
- **EFMP enrollment is done through your gaining unit and DoDEA**, not ACS. ACS is the local liaison and advocate only.
- **AER emergency loans** require command-sponsorship approval; plan for 24-hour minimum processing.
