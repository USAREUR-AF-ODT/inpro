## Content Intro

## Temporary Housing Overview

With certain military moves, you may find yourself looking for housing that’s a little less permanent before making long-term arrangements. There are several temporary housing options that may be right for you. Explore the information about temporary housing facilities including allowances, lodging rates and eligibility.

## Subtopics

Temporary Lodging Facility (TLF)

[Make reservations for temporary lodging](http://army.dodlodging.net/propertys/Wiesbaden-Lodge), and be sure to notify them if you have a pet.

Please be aware if you stay off post, there may be an extra fee charged at hotels for pets.

Make arrangements for your pets. Families are allowed up to two pets in housing. Dogs and cats only. No exotic animals. Your pet will need to have a European microchip, a USDA/veterinarian certification, and an up-to-date rabies vaccine. Contact the [VETERINARY CLINIC](https://phc.amedd.army.mil/organization/Pages/VtfDetails.aspx?VtfID=23) and the airline for more information on prohibited breeds and customs.

Our typical PCS-In military family resides with us 30 or fewer days, subject to on-post/off-post housing availability.

The Wiesbaden Army Lodge is located in the Hainerberg Housing Area on Mississippi Str. 45 Bldg 7890, 65189 Wiesbaden.

The Main Gate is located at Washington Strasse 75, 65189 Wiesbaden. Once through the main gate, continue straight to Texasstrasse and make your first right after the PX parking lot and then a left onto MIssissippistrasse and continue straight.

Please request this accommodation when making the reservation. If ADA rooms are not available, an appropriate ADA room will be coordinated with a local Wiesbaden hotel.

Official Visitor Reservations are accepted 180 days in advance of arrival, Unoffical Visitor Reservations are accepted 7 days in advance of arrival for Friday, Saturday and Sunday.

Monday-Thursday UV walk-ins are accepted after 6:00 p.m.

Reservations are accepted 24/7 at DSN 314-548-9707/9700 or COM 011 49 611-723-760-9707/9700, or you may send an email to [reservations@wiesbadenlodge.com](mailto:reservations@wiesbadenlodge.com).

Website: [http://army.dodlodging.net/propertys/Wiesbaden-Lodge](http://army.dodlodging.net/propertys/Wiesbaden-Lodge)

The Army Lodge: 164 guest rooms and suites with 36 pet friendly rooms. Contact the lodge directly for reservations.

_**Temporary Lodging / Special Needs**_

A limited number of ADA rooms are available. Please request this accommodation when making the reservation. If ADA rooms are not available, an appropriate ADA room will be coordinated with a local Wiesbaden hotel. Bonafide service animals stay free of charge and guests are required to validate at the time of their arrival.

_**Directions to Wiesbaden Lodge, Mississippi Str.**_

- Connect to Autobahn 66
- Take Wiesbaden Erbenheim exit from Autobahn 66. (This will be B455)
- Stay straight on B455. (Do not take the second Erbenheim exit)
- Coming into Wiesbaden, you will come to an underpass on left, Merge into FAR RIGHT LANE; you will then merge on to New York Strasse.
- Stay straight, through traffic light; take next right on to Texas Strasse, and take next left on to Mississippi Strasse.
- You will proceed up Mississippi Strasse for approximately 150 meters, the Wiesbaden Lodge in on left.
- If dropping off family members or luggage please make use of the covered drop-off in front of entrance to hotel
