**Army Lodging**

Army Lodging Hospitality Programs is passionate about providing quality hotel accommodations to America’s Soldiers, Families, Retirees, and civilians. Army Lodging is your choice for official and leisure travel. Staying at Army Lodging also offers the convenience of other on post amenities and services including Dining, Golf, Bowling, Fitness, Outdoor Recreation, the Library and The Exchange. With over 1,800 guest rooms at 16 locations worldwide, Army Lodging strives to provide our guests with a consistent quality experience. Army Lodging is available to Active Duty, Retirees, DoD Civilians (GS & NAF) and their sponsored guests.

Military personnel on orders must receive a Certificate or Statement of Non-availability from Army Lodging prior to staying in an off-post hotel in order to be reimbursed for lodging expenses.

Reservations

To book a room at the Wiesbaden Lodge, please fill out the [Reservation Form](https://wiesbaden.armymwr.com/download_file/view/1f89881e-839c-4b47-89b8-36c5fb6f52e7/9704) and email it to [reservations@army.mil](mailto:reservations@army.mil?subject=Reservation%20Request), or give us a call at [+49 (0) 611-723-760](tel:+49611723760) _._ We are available 24/7 and ready to assist you with your lodging needs.

For those on official travel orders, reservations may be made up to a year in advance of arrival. Orders must be presented upon arrival. For Space-A and leisure travel, reservations may be booked seven days in advance of arrival. Extensions are granted on a day-to-day basis. Any exception to Space-A policy will be based on room availability.

**Guaranteed Reservations and Booking Changes**

All guaranteed reservations (those booked with a credit card) will be released at 6 p.m. If your arrival time or date has changed, please be sure to call and cancel or adjust your reservation prior to 6 p.m. on the original anticipated arrival date. Failure to cancel or adjust your reservation will result in a no-show fee equal to one night's stay and the reservation will be cancelled.

**Non-Guaranteed Reservations and Booking Changes**

All non-guaranteed reservations (those booked without a credit card) will be released at 6 p.m. If your arrival time or date has changed, please be sure to call and cancel or adjust your reservation prior to 6 p.m. on the original anticipated arrival date. Failure to cancel or adjust your reservation will result in cancellation of your reservation.

Pet Policy & Fees

Pets are welcomed at the Wiesbaden Lodge!

In accordance with the **USAG Wiesbaden Command Policy Letter #77 Pet Policy**, USAG-WI residents are authorized to have pets in AFH and Army Lodging in any one of the following combinations:

1) Three small pets

2) Two small and one medium

3) Two small and one large

4) Two medium

5) One medium and one large

or

6) Two large

a. Small dogs are those less than or equal to 30 pounds or 14 kilograms, such as

Chihuahua; Bichon Frise; terriers; Shih Tzu; Pomeranian; or Pug. For this section, cats are

classified as small pets.

b. Medium size dogs are those 31 to 54 pounds or 15 to 24 kilograms, such as Basset

Hounds; Cocker Spaniel; Beagle; or Bulldog.

c. Large dogs are those greater than 55 pounds or 25 kilograms, such as Labrador; Golden

Retriever; German Shepherd; Husky; or Mastiff.

**Fees**

There is a one-time, non-reimbursable deep cleaning charge assessed in the amount of $50 and a $10 per day, per pet fee. Per our pet policy, pet(s) must be kenneled in order to receive service. Room service and/or inspection of pet rooms is required, at a minimum of every three days.

\*Non-registered pets or pets discovered in a non-pet friendly room, will incur fines as per our policy.

**Guests are required to present pet vaccination records upon check-in.**

Check-In and Check-Out

Guests may check in after 3 p.m. Check out must be prior to 11 a.m. or subsequent charges may be incurred.

Payments

We accept major credit cards, U.S. cash, and personal checks. However, we require a valid credit card to guarantee room reservations. If you are unable to present a credit card, payment for the duration of your stay must be paid at the time of arrival.

All guests on official orders (PCS/TDY) must present a valid Government Travel Card or personal credit card upon check-in for all Army Lodging facilities.

For the local Finance Office contact information and a comprehensive PCS checklist, please [click here](https://wiesbaden.armymwr.com/download_file/view/5aa13890-428b-4ede-b34f-3c7b232c4d88/9704).

Frequently Asked Questions

**1. What are the Room Rates? Answer:**

Nightly rates can vary by month but will never exceed the maximum per diem allowance. View the current per diem allowances here:

[Foreign Per Diem Rates In U.S. Dollars](https://aoprals.state.gov/web920/per_diem_action.asp?MenuHide=1&CountryCode=1089)

**2. May U.S. Army Retirees residing or traveling in Germany stay at a lodge? Answer:**  Retirees residing or traveling in Germany are authorized to use Army Lodging facilities on a Space-Available basis.

**3. Is there complimentary breakfast? Answer:** Sunrise Start Breakfast is available for registered guests only and included in the room rate.

**_Breakfast Hours:_**

Monday-Friday                                             0600-0900                                                           Weekends & US Federal Holidays:          0800-1000

**4\. Where is the closest airport? Answer:** Frankfurt International Airport is approximately a 30-minute drive from the Wiesbaden Lodge.

**5\. Is there an airport shuttle service available?** Unfortunately, there is not, but the Wiesbaden Lodge can coordinate local Taxi Services (rates may vary). We are also an approximate 20-minute walk from the Wiesbaden Hauptbahnhof (train station).

**6\. Where can I find photos of the hotel? Answer:** Please see below.

**7\. Where can I find directions to the hotel? Answer:** We are located at Mississippistrasse 45, 65189 Wiesbaden.

The Lodge is located near the Wiesbaden Entertainment Center on Hainerberg Housing.

The Main Gate (ACP) is located at Washington Strasse 67, 65189 Wiesbaden. Once through the main gate, continue straight to Texasstrasse and make your first right after the PX parking lot and then a left onto Mississippistrasse and continue straight.

Click [here](https://maps.app.goo.gl/tu1Q4BtvTpnAfka68) for directions.
