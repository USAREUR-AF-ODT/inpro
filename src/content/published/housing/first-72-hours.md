## What

This is the "if you only read one thing before you land" page. The run sheet below assumes you're command-sponsored and have working-day arrivals. Adjust for weekends.

## Day 1; arrival day

1. **Land and collect bags.** If you flew Patriot Express, there's a shuttle from the Ramstein/Frankfurt reception center. If you flew commercial, your sponsor should meet you; if they don't, call the Army Lodge at Clay Kaserne to confirm your reservation.
2. **Check into the Wiesbaden Army Lodge** on Clay Kaserne. Start your TLA clock. Keep the Army Lodge receipt; Finance needs it.
3. **Let your sponsor know you're on the ground.** Sleep.

## Day 2; unit and ID

1. **Sign in to your unit.** This is the earliest thing your reporting chain needs; the TLA clock runs from when you're officially in. Bring orders and dep ID.
2. **Start CAC/DBIDS at the ID Card Center.** If you need to re-issue CACs for Family members or spouse dependent IDs, get in the queue early. This is slow.
3. **Housing Services Office (HSO), Bldg 1023 W.** Call ahead; Mon/Wed/Fri are walk-in, Tue/Thu are appointment-only. Every accompanied arrival goes here before signing a lease. (See the [HSO entry](/entries/housing/housing-services-office).)

## Day 3; finance and vehicles

1. **Finance office.** TLA paperwork, COLA/OHA setup, travel voucher filing. Bring your Army Lodge receipts, orders, and PCS travel claim.
2. **DES Registration of Motor Vehicles.** If your POV shipped, this comes later (4-6 weeks typically). If you're buying local, register it here; $45/year, safety inspection required first. (See the [vehicle registration entry](/entries/vehicle/vehicle-registration).)
3. **Wiesbaden Army Health Clinic** enrollment, if you haven't already. TRICARE Overseas Prime transfer takes 5-10 business days; start it now.

## What you can safely defer

- **Anmeldung** (German residency registration); you have 14 days but do it in week 2, not week 1.
- **German driver's license conversion**; you can drive on your US license for up to 6 months under SOFA.
- **Off-post exploration**; do it after you have reliable transport.
- **SOFA stamp in passport**; happens within 90 days, not immediately.

## Gotchas

- **Tuesday is HSO appointment-only.** Planning to walk in Tuesday is the single most common Day-2 mistake.
- **The ID Card Center takes walk-ins but waits are 2-4 hours at PCS peak** (May-August). Try early morning on a weekday if you need multiple dependents re-badged.
- **TLA is 10 days initial, extendable.** Don't assume 60 days; you earn extensions by documenting active housing search through HSO.
- **You will be jetlagged and dumb.** Write things down. Bring a paper checklist. Use the phone note app.
- **Don't try to do all three days back-to-back on Day 1.** You'll forget something.

## Source notes

The run sheet above is synthesized from the garrison in-processing page, the 2024 Welcome Packet, and MilitaryOneSource's Wiesbaden check-in procedures. Timings reflect the typical command-sponsored accompanied arrival pattern; unaccompanied Soldiers and civilians have a slightly compressed schedule (barracks assignment replaces Army Lodge).
