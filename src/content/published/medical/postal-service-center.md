****** Postal Service Center
| U.S. Army Garrison Wiesbaden ******

**** Smart Lockers at the Clay Postal Center are now operational[Smart lockers July 2025
photo.jpeg]! ****
These lockers are secure, automated, and located at the Clay Kaserne Postal Service Center.
Customers can retrieve packages 24/7 without waiting in line at the package counter. Find out more
about how they work in the informational pamphlet here.

Mission: The USAG Wiesbaden Postal Service Center provides an array of service to include Priority
boxes, Priority envelopes, Federal Express services, computer and printers for generating e-custom
forms, money orders, stamps and US and international postage for outgoing mail.

Location: Clay Kaserne, Bldg. 1030

Finance/Mailing-Outoperating hours:
Mon. & Fri: 11 a.m. - 2 p.m.
Tues. - Thurs: 11 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.
If you have five or more packages to ship, you can schedule an appointment from Monday through
Friday between 9:30 am till 10:30 am.
Please ensure all your packages are properly prepared for shipping.
Phone: DSN 548-1505/1512; Civ: (0611) 143-548-1505/1512; Closed U.S. Federal Holidays​​​​​​​

U.S. Postal Service customers shipping packages overseas must fill out customs forms online. This
includes mailing packages from Europe to the U.S., as well as from the U.S. to Europe. For details,
contact the post office or visit the USPS_customs_page.
Non-Registered_Account_Users
    * For senders without a registered account with USPS.com. All information must be filled in each
      time used.
Registered_Account_Users
    * For senders with a registered account with USPS.com. Registration is free. Information will be
      saved for future use.
Click-n-Ship_Account_Users
    * For senders with a registered direct postage payment account with USPS.com. 
    * If customers are having difficulty paying online at the USPS website, they can come to the
      post office and have their name and account registered in the "Click-N-Ship Exception List" 
      and within 72 hours their payment option will be active.
