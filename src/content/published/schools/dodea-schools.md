## What

DoDEA Wiesbaden operates four schools serving command-sponsored Family members in the USAG Wiesbaden footprint. Registration routes through the School Liaison Office (SLO) and individual school registrars.

Tuition is free for ADFMs. DA Civilian dependents may need Tuition-Paying Civilian (TPC) status depending on the parent's employment category. Contractor dependents typically don't get DoDEA access.

## The four schools

### Wiesbaden High School (WHS)
**Grades 9–12.** On **Clay Kaserne**. Housing catchment covers on-post and surrounding Wiesbaden-area housing for all high school students.

- **JROTC**: active; Warrior Battalion, well-regarded
- **AP offerings**: ~15 AP courses typical
- **Athletics**: football, basketball, soccer, track, wrestling, etc. — travels across USAREUR
- **DoD graduation requirements**: similar to US states; DoDEA diploma is accepted by US colleges
- **School colors**: Burgundy/Gold

Address + map: on Clay Kaserne; entrance via a base pass.

### Wiesbaden Middle School (WMS)
**Grades 6–8.** On **Hainerberg Housing.** Catchment: Hainerberg, Aukamm-area middle schoolers.

- Transition year programming
- Solid STEM and humanities
- Sports: 7th-8th grade intramural + some varsity

### Aukamm Elementary School (AES)
**Pre-K through 5.** On **Aukamm Housing.** Catchment: Aukamm residents; occasional spillover.

- Pre-K and K programs
- Full-day Kindergarten
- English as a Second Language (ESL) support for international Family members

### Hainerberg Elementary School (HES)
**Pre-K through 5.** On **Hainerberg Housing.** Catchment: Hainerberg residents, Newman Village, Crestview.

- Similar scope to Aukamm
- Strong parent community
- Mostly US-origin student body

## How catchment works

Which school your child attends is determined by your housing address, not by choice. DoDEA Wiesbaden is single-district; students are assigned to the nearest appropriate school based on residence.

If you live **on-post** at Hainerberg → HES (elementary) and WMS (middle)
If you live **on-post** at Aukamm → AES
If you live **off-post** in Wiesbaden proper → whichever elementary is closest, or WMS/WHS
If you live **off-post in Taunusstein/Bierstadt** → possibly bussed to Wiesbaden schools; confirm with SLO

### School bus
DoDEA runs buses to Wiesbaden-area housing. Routes posted at school start. Bus stops at standard housing-area pick-ups; off-post bus eligibility requires specific criteria.

**Off-post parents often drive**, especially if the bus route is inefficient.

## Registration process

### 30–60 days before arrival
1. **Contact SLO**: introduce yourself at **DSN 548-9201** and request a pre-enrollment packet
2. **Gather documents**:
   - Child's birth certificate
   - Sponsor's PCS orders (dependent authorization)
   - DEERS enrollment verification
   - Immunization records (translated to English if from German providers)
   - Most recent report card or transcript
   - IEP / 504 Plan (if applicable)
3. **Decide on school**: verify your expected housing → confirmed school assignment

### After arrival
1. **Visit the specific school's registrar**
2. Complete enrollment forms on-site
3. Schedule kindergarten screening or grade-placement assessments if needed
4. **For high school**: counselor meeting to set course sequence

### Enrollment windows
- **Late spring for next-year enrollment** (April–May for August start)
- **Mid-year PCS**: register within 5 school days of arrival
- **Summer arrival**: office hours limited in late July; contact SLO first

## DoDEA Wiesbaden calendar

- **First day**: around late August (follows DoDEA system calendar)
- **Fall break**: 1 week in October
- **Winter break**: 2 weeks, roughly Dec 22 – Jan 6
- **Spring break**: 1 week in March/April (varies with Easter)
- **Last day**: mid-June
- **Summer break**: 10 weeks

DoDEA calendar does NOT align with German public-school calendar. DoDEA parents often plan trips during breaks that happen when Germans are still in school (fall break, winter break).

## IEP / 504 / Special Education

See the [EFMP Enrollment](/entries/family/efmp-enrollment) and [School Liaison Office](/entries/family/school-liaison-office) entries for detail. Summary:

- Send IEP to SLO 30+ days before arrival
- Transfer IEP meeting within 10 school days of arrival
- DoDEA honors most accommodations with German-specific adjustments
- **504 plans transfer similarly** via the school's 504 coordinator
- **Gifted identification**: DoDEA has a gifted program; request screening early

## Language instruction

DoDEA teaches **German** as a second language in most elementary schools (exposure) and middle school. WHS offers German I–IV plus AP German. Other languages (Spanish, French) available at WHS.

## Co-curricular and extracurricular

- **WHS** has ~30 clubs: robotics, drama, music, academic teams, student government
- **WMS** has school-spirit programming, yearbook, drama, intramurals
- **Elementary**: PTA-run enrichment, after-school care via CYS

## Off-post alternatives

Some families choose off-post German or international schools instead of DoDEA:

### German public schools
- **Grundschule** (elementary 1–4)
- **Gymnasium** (high school, 5–12/13, university-prep)
- **Realschule** / **Hauptschule** (alternative paths)
- **Gesamtschule** (comprehensive)
- **Kosten (cost)**: free for residents; open enrollment based on Anmeldung
- **Language**: German-primary; some schools offer bilingual tracks

### International / private schools
- **Frankfurt International School** (45 min drive; tuition ~€25,000/year)
- **Metropolitan School Frankfurt** (tuition ~€20,000/year)
- **Internationale Schule Wiesbaden** (newer; tuition ~€15,000/year)

DoDEA kids can transfer to international schools mid-tour; paperwork differs.

## Gotchas

- **DoDEA catchment is rigid.** On-post housing = on-post school. Don't assume you can swap.
- **Immunization records in German won't be accepted.** Translate or have ARHC re-issue in English.
- **IEP transfer lag**: 10-school-day meeting is a ceiling, not a floor. Push for earlier if needed.
- **High school credit transfers**: some state's graduation requirements don't map 1:1 to DoDEA. The counselor smooths this out; start early.
- **Sports eligibility**: WHS and WMS compete across USAREUR; physicals are required; schedule 30 days in advance.
- **Kindergarten age cut-off**: DoDEA cut-off is Oct 1 (child must be 5 by then). Some states use Sep 1; don't assume.
- **Transcripts need to be official.** Unofficial PDFs bounce; order official sealed from losing school.
- **Summer arrival tips**: register via email with SLO in June to beat the August rush.
- **Kids' German exposure**: if your kids attend DoDEA, they'll develop ZERO organic German exposure. Consider after-school German or homestay trips to supplement.
